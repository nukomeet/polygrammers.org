Polygrammers

We are polyglot programmers

About

Polygrammers is an initiatve to promote polyglot programming. We organize meetups in various cities around the globe.

Our meetups are up to 100 minutes long. Each meetup has the following plan:

  • HackerNews articles discussion (20 min)
  • 2-3 talks (15 min each)
  • Show Off - present your projects (5 min each)
  • Beer Time
